FIR - Five In a Row for PalmOS(R)

What

Fir is a "tic-tac-toe"-like two-player game, where each player has a designated symbol. The objective is to get five symbols in a line, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. The players take turns placing their symbol in an unused square. The player who achieves the objective first has won.

How to play

Symbols: You are X, the computer is O.
Placing symbols: Tap on an empty square.
Hint: You can get a hint to where to place your symbol by tapping the "Hint" button.
Auto Play: You can watch the computer play against itself by tapping the "Auto" button. To turn auto play off, tap button or board again.
Start new game: start a new game by tapping the "New Game" button.

For which platform

Platforms running PalmOS(R) ROM versions 3.0 and higher. Color is supported for devices that have color.
